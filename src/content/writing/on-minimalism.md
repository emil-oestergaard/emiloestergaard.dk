There is a small genre of personal site that gets out of its own way. No hero
gradients, no carousel, no animated cards. Just a column of text, a clear
hierarchy, and whitespace that tells you the author trusts the words to do the
work.

## The signal and the noise

The signal is the writing. Everything else — typefaces, color, layout — is
noise you spend deliberately. A good personal site reduces noise until what
remains feels inevitable.

## A working checklist

- One column, comfortable measure
- One or two typefaces, chosen on purpose
- Two themes, both legible
- No animation that can't justify itself
